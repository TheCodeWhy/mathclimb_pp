Updated as of September 1, 2023

personal information when you use our service through this privacy policy ("Policy").

We don't collect any personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") from our App users.

We take commercially reasonable measures to protect your Personal Information, but we can't guarantee absolute security.

Links to External Websites

The App may have links to external websites not operated by us. We don't control the content, privacy policies,
or practices of these third-party sites and services. We strongly recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not meant for individuals under 13 years old. We don't knowingly collect or request Personal Information
from children under 13. We use trusted third-party services to offer additional features and enhance the user experience.
These third parties may collect and process personal information, but we've taken steps to ensure they comply with legal privacy requirements.
We suggest reviewing their privacy policies (mentioned above) to understand their practices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
